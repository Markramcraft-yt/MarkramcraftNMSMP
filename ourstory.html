Markramcraft Netcom SMP was founded in 2026 by a Markramcraft YouTuber named Markramcraft.

With the help of a dedicated team of moderators and volunteers, Markramcraft launched the server in early 2026. The server quickly gained popularity, attracting players from all over the world who were looking for a friendly and supportive Minecraft community.

Over the years, Markramcraft Netcom SMP has continued to grow and evolve. The server has added new game modes, features, and events to keep players engaged and entertained. The community has also become more active and supportive, with players forming friendships and collaborating on amazing builds and projects.

While Markramcraft is still alive and managing the server, he remains committed to providing a positive and engaging experience for all players.
